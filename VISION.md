# ♔ Chess AI Coach

## Vision

> Chess AI Coach is not another chess GUI.

It is a personal chess trainer that understands your games,
explains your mistakes,
remembers your weaknesses
and helps you improve over time.

---

# Mission

Teach people chess the same way a human coach would.

Not by showing engine evaluations.

But by explaining ideas.

The player should always understand:

- Why was this move good?
- Why was it bad?
- What was the opponent threatening?
- What should I look for next time?

The goal is understanding, not memorization.

---

# Philosophy

Stockfish knows the best move.

AI explains WHY.

Those are two different jobs.

Chess AI Coach combines them.

---

# Design Principles

## Explain, don't judge.

Never simply say

❌ Blunder

Instead say

"This move leaves your bishop without protection,
allowing Black to gain a tempo."

---

## Be encouraging.

The coach should never sound arrogant.

Bad:

"You blundered."

Good:

"This is a common mistake.
Let's see why it happened."

---

## Every feature should teach something.

Graphs exist to explain.

Evaluation exists to explain.

Best move exists to explain.

Nothing exists only because Chess.com has it.

---

## Context is everything.

The coach never explains a move in isolation.

It understands:

- opening
- move history
- current position
- evaluation
- best line
- player's previous mistakes
